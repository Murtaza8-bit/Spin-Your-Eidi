# 🌙 EidSurpriseWheel – Eid Mubarak Card & Eidi Spin

A responsive and interactive Eid experience built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Users open a festive Eid envelope, receive a greeting card, spin a wheel to reveal their Eidi amount, and claim it instantly via WhatsApp!

### 🔗 [Click Here to Play](https://murtaza8-bit.github.io/eid-surprise-wheel/)

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🎁 **Eid Envelope Animation**: Tap to open a beautiful red envelope with a flap animation
- 🌙 **Greeting Card**: Festive Eid Mubarak card with Arabic text and gold shimmer effects
- 🎡 **Eidi Spin Wheel**: Spin once to reveal your Eidi — Rs. 1000, Rs. 500, Rs. 10, or Rs. 0
- 💬 **WhatsApp Claim Button**: Winners tap one button and WhatsApp opens with a pre-written claim message
- 🎊 **Confetti Effect**: Celebration confetti bursts for big wins
- 🌸 **Dynamic Background**: Animated pink gradient with floating particles and emojis
- 📐 **Responsive Design**: Works smoothly on all screen sizes and mobile devices

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **HTML** – Structural backbone
- **CSS** – Visual presentation & animations
- **JavaScript** – Dynamic behavior, SVG envelope & WhatsApp integration

---

## 🎮 How It Works

1. Open the link and tap the red envelope 🎁
2. Read the Eid Mubarak greeting card 🌙
3. Press **Spin** — you only get **one attempt!** 🎡
4. Won something? Tap **Claim Your Eidi on WhatsApp** and send the message! 💬
